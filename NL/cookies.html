Cookie Policy

Last updated: December 26, 2024

1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how Diaeta (operated by Pierre Abou-Zeid) uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website https://diaeta.be. This policy should be read alongside our Privacy Policy.

By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree to our use of cookies, you should set your browser settings accordingly or refrain from using our website.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website owners.

Cookies can be:

  • Session cookies: Temporary cookies that are deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent cookies: Cookies that remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them
  • First-party cookies: Cookies set by our website
  • Third-party cookies: Cookies set by external services we use

3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

3.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation, access to secure areas, and form submissions. The website cannot function properly without these cookies.

  • Purpose: Website functionality, security, and user session management
  • Duration: Session or up to 1 year
  • Can be disabled: No (website will not function properly)

3.2 Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously.

  • Purpose: Website analytics, performance monitoring, and user behavior analysis
  • Duration: Up to 26 months
  • Can be disabled: Yes (through cookie settings)
  • Third-party services: Google Analytics

3.3 Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization, such as remembering your preferences and settings.

  • Purpose: User preferences, language settings, and enhanced functionality
  • Duration: Up to 1 year
  • Can be disabled: Yes (some features may not work optimally)

3.4 Marketing Cookies

These cookies are used to track visitors across websites to display relevant and engaging advertisements.

  • Purpose: Marketing and advertising optimization
  • Duration: Up to 2 years
  • Can be disabled: Yes (through cookie settings)

5. Third-Party Cookies

We use the following third-party services that may set cookies:

5.1 Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect information about your use of our website, including:

  • Pages you visit
  • Time spent on each page
  • How you reached our website
  • Your general location (country/city level)
  • Device and browser information

This information is used to improve our website and provide better user experience. Google Analytics data is processed in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have several options for managing cookies:

6.1 Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • View what cookies are stored on your device
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Block cookies from being set
  • Set preferences for different types of cookies

Please note that blocking certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

6.2 Cookie Consent Management

You can manage your cookie preferences through our cookie consent banner or by clicking "Cookie Settings" in our footer. This allows you to:

  • Accept or reject different categories of cookies
  • Change your preferences at any time
  • Withdraw consent for previously accepted cookies

6.3 Third-Party Opt-Outs

For third-party cookies, you can opt out directly through the service providers:

  • Google Analytics: Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR and Belgian data protection laws, you have the following rights regarding cookies:

  • Right to be informed: We provide clear information about our use of cookies
  • Right to consent: You can choose which cookies to accept
  • Right to withdraw consent: You can change your cookie preferences at any time
  • Right to object: You can object to the use of certain cookies

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on our website and updating the "Last updated" date.
