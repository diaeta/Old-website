To eat is a necessity, but to eat intelligently is an art.

We will help you master this art. Don't give up on your favorite food, make it healthier.

Thanks to nutrigenetics, you can ask for custom-made nutritional advice, based on your genetic profile.

In 480 BC. AD, Hippocrates noted that "positive health requires knowledge of the basic constitution of man." It was just an old way of saying that we can't achieve optimal health without knowing our genes. We now know that specific variations in our genes can explain how we will respond to the foods, drinks, and supplements we consume.

Personalized nutritional advice

Daily, how do you combine nutrition and health ? How do we identify eating behaviors that harm our health, vitality and balance? The first choice is mainly to give yourself the time to get information and get advice from an expert.

The dietitian is the only recognized nutritional specialist whose title is protected by the Royal Decree of 19/02/1997. He is therefore the only one (besides the nutritionist doctor) who is authorized to give nutritional advice. It is also the only one that allows reimbursement of consultations by health insurance funds. (Check with your health insurance)
